## Short Description (80 chars max)

## Long Description

BodyMind OS is the business tool built specifically for independent massage therapists and bodyworkers. No multi-staff spa features you'll never use. No commission on bookings. Just the tools you need to run a professional solo practice.

**What it does:**
- **Booking Page** — Share a link. Clients book themselves, fill out intake forms, and you get notified. No more texting back and forth.
- **Digital Intake Forms** — Health history with automatic contraindication flagging. Know about DVT, pregnancy, osteoporosis, or cancer treatment before the session starts.
- **SOAP Session Notes** — Standard clinical documentation format. Record what you did, what worked, pressure preferences, areas of tension. On your phone between sessions.
- **CE Compliance Tracker** — Log continuing education hours. See exactly how many hours you need and when your renewal deadline is. Missing CE = losing your license.
- **Client CRM** — Contact info, visit history, tags. Know which clients haven't been back in 90 days.
- **Revenue Dashboard** — Income, retention, no-show rates. See the numbers that matter.
- **Calendar** — Availability management, recurring blocks, buffer time between sessions.

**Pricing:**
- Starter: $0/mo (booking, calendar, CRM — up to 20 clients)
- Pro: $29/mo (intake forms, SOAP notes, CE tracker, unlimited clients)
- Studio: $49/mo (multi-calendar, split payments)

**Tech:**
- Zero dependencies. Vanilla JavaScript, localStorage persistence.
- Works in any browser. Mobile-first design.
- No account required. No server. No data leaving your browser.
- License key format: BOMD-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX
