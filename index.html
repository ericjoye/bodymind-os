Frequently Asked Questions

"I'm not tech-savvy. Is this hard to set up?"

No setup required. No account to create. Open the app, enter your name, and start using the calendar. If you can send a text message, you can use BodyMind OS.

"Does this work on my phone?"

Yes — it's built for phone-first use. Take notes between sessions, check your calendar while walking to a client, and update client records on your phone. No app store download needed.

"Fresha is free. Why would I pay $29?"

Fresha handles booking and payments, but it doesn't do SOAP session notes, contraindication tracking, digital intake forms, or CE compliance tracking. You'd still need paper forms, a notebook for session notes, and a spreadsheet for CE hours. BodyMind OS replaces all of that in one tool.

"What if I stop paying? Do I lose my data?"

Your data stays in your browser. If you stop paying, you keep access to the Starter features (calendar, booking page, client CRM up to 20 clients) forever. Only the Pro features (intake forms, SOAP notes, CE tracker) are gated behind the license key.

"I almost lost my license because I missed a CE deadline. How does the tracker work?"

Set your state in Settings, and the CE tracker shows exactly how many hours you need, how many you've logged, and when your renewal deadline is. Log hours on your phone after each course. You'll never wonder "am I compliant?" again.

"I work alone and share a space with one other therapist. Do I need Studio or Pro?"

If you're the only therapist using the system, Pro is the right fit — $29/mo for intake forms, SOAP notes, CE tracker, unlimited clients. Studio ($49/mo) adds multi-calendar and split payment tracking if you sublet to other therapists under your business.

"Can clients book through the app?"

Yes. Share your booking page link (book a session), clients pick a service, choose a time, and fill their health history before they arrive. No more texting back and forth or waiting for phone calls.

"Can I try Pro before paying?"

Yes — Start free with Starter (no credit card needed). If you decide to upgrade, Pro is $29/mo with a 14-day money-back guarantee. If it doesn't work for you, send an email and you get a full refund.
