## How It Works

**1. Clients book themselves.** Share your booking page link. Clients pick a time, fill out their health intake form, and you get a notification. No more texting back and forth.

**2. Intake forms flag risks automatically.** Every new client fills out a health history. The form highlights contraindications (DVT, pregnancy, osteoporosis, cancer treatment) so you know before the session starts.

**3. Session notes in SOAP format.** Record what you did, what worked, pressure preferences, areas of tension. Standard clinical documentation — on your phone between sessions.

**4. CE tracking by state.** Log your continuing education hours. See exactly how many hours you need and when your renewal deadline is. Missing CE = losing your license = cannot work.

**5. Revenue dashboard.** See income, client retention, and no-show rates. Know which clients haven't been back in 90 days.

## FAQ

**"I'm not tech-savvy. Is this going to be hard to set up?"**
No install, no account needed for your clients. Open it in any browser, enter your availability, share your booking page link. If you can use Google Calendar, you can use this.

**"Fresha is free. Why should I pay?"**
Fresha is free because they monetize payment processing. More importantly, Fresha has zero massage-specific features — no intake forms, no contraindication flags, no SOAP notes, no CE tracking. You'd still need paper forms and a notebook. BodyMind OS replaces all of that.

**"What if I stop paying?"**
Your data stays in your browser. Export it anytime. No lock-in, no hostage situation.

**"Does this work on my phone?"**
 Yes. Mobile-first design. Works on any device with a browser — no app store download needed.

**"I only have 15 clients. Do I need this?"**
If 15 clients means 15 intake forms, 15 session histories, and 15 people who might forget their appointment — yes. The Starter plan is free and handles up to 20 clients. Start there, upgrade when you're ready.

**"What about CE requirements? My state is different."**
CE tracker lets you set your state's required hours and renewal date. It tracks completed hours and shows exactly how many you need. Currently supports all 49 regulated states.

**"Is my client data safe?"**
All data is stored in your browser's localStorage. Nothing is sent to a server. No account, no cloud, no data breach risk. (This also means no multi-device sync — use one browser for now.)

**"Can clients book without my help?"**
Yes. Your booking page is a shareable link. Clients pick an available time, fill out their intake form, and you get notified. No back-and-forth texting.
